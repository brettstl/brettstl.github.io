Digital Measurement Planning

The digital measurement plan is used to document and follow your top line business goals as they dictact what specific data you should collect, how you will collect it, and how you should analyize this data. The measurement plan is the most critical step to turning digital analytics into a machine for making great business decisions.

IMO, there should be classes dedicated to this. I would love to take a class on measurement planning. I am lame.
